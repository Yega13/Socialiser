## Claude's Ideas

### HIGH PRIORITY (Big Impact, Builds Trust)

10. **Onboarding tour** — 3-step walkthrough on first login: connect platform → upload media → post. Users should post within 60 seconds. Every competitor fails at this — Metricool takes forever, Buffer is confusing. This alone sets us apart.

11. **Post preview by platform** — show exactly how your post will look on Instagram vs YouTube vs TikTok BEFORE posting. Side-by-side phone mockups. Nobody does this well. Users currently post blindly and hope it looks right.

12. **Post history with status** — simple feed showing: what you posted, when, where, and did it succeed or fail. With clear error messages when something breaks. Buffer's silent failures are their #1 complaint. We show "Posted ✓" or "Failed — here's why" every time.

13. **Media library** — upload once, reuse anywhere. With folders, search, and drag-to-compose. Metricool gets destroyed for not having this ("like a car without wheels"). Publer's is broken (can't sort or export). We build it right.

14. **Transparent billing page** — pricing page that's dead simple. Renewal reminder emails 7 days before charge. One-click cancel. No double-click traps. Every single competitor gets roasted for sneaky billing. This is free trust.

15. **Caption character counter per platform** — Instagram: 2,200 chars, X: 280 chars, TikTok: 4,000 chars. Show live counter that turns red when approaching limit. Auto-truncation warning. Simple but nobody does it cleanly.

16. **Keyboard shortcuts** — Ctrl+Enter to post, Ctrl+S to save draft, Ctrl+P for preview. Power users will love this. Makes the "fast" principle real.

### MEDIUM PRIORITY (Good Features, Not Urgent)

17. **Best time to post** — analyze when your audience is most active, suggest optimal posting times. Metricool's most-praised feature. We can add this once we have analytics data.

18. **Draft auto-save** — never lose a post in progress. Auto-save every 10 seconds. Buffer users complain about losing saved changes. We never lose your work.

19. **Bulk scheduling / CSV upload** — Publer's most-loved feature. Upload 100+ posts at once via CSV. Power users and agencies need this.

20. **Monthly email summary** — "Here's how your content performed this month" — sent to email without needing to log in. Metricool has this and users love it.

21. **Timezone selector** — let users set their timezone for scheduling. Simple, useful, expected.

22. **Dark/light mode** — already built ✅

23. **Multi-format export** — download your analytics as PDF or image for client reports. Metricool charges for this on higher tiers. We could include it free.

24. **Hashtag suggestions** — when typing #, suggest popular/relevant hashtags. Lighter version of Metricool's $600/mo hashtag tracker. Free, useful, not overcomplicated.

25. **Post templates** — save frequently used caption structures. "New product launch", "Behind the scenes", etc. Saves time for users who post similar content regularly.

### LOW PRIORITY (Nice to Have, Later)

26. **Team collaboration** — invite team members, approval workflows. Only needed when we target agencies. Not MVP.

27. **Calendar view** — visual monthly calendar of scheduled posts. Drag-and-drop to reschedule. Later's best feature. Add when scheduling is mature.

28. **Content recycling** — auto-repost evergreen content on a schedule. Publer users love this. Niche but powerful for businesses.

29. **Link-in-bio tool** — everyone has this. Not our differentiator. Add eventually but don't prioritize.

30. **Browser extension** — schedule content from anywhere on the web. Nice but not essential.

31. **Mobile app** — eventually essential but not MVP. Web-first, mobile later.

32. **Competitor tracking** — see what competitors are posting and how it performs. Metricool's feature. Cool but complex and not creator-focused.

### NEW — 10 More Ideas (Claude Round 2)

33. **Smart image auto-resize** — upload one image, we auto-crop to each platform's ideal dimensions (IG square 1:1, Story/Reel 9:16, YT thumbnail 16:9, X header 3:1). Creators currently use Canva JUST to resize. We eliminate that step entirely.

34. **Instagram first comment scheduling** — option to schedule a first comment with your hashtags, separate from the caption. This is the #1 most-requested feature across ALL social media tools. Instagram's algorithm actually prefers hashtags in comments, not captions. Nobody does this reliably.

35. **Video thumbnail picker** — choose a frame from your video as the thumbnail, or upload a custom one. Metricool literally CAN'T do this for YouTube Shorts. Creators care deeply about thumbnails — it's the first thing people see.

36. **Post success/failure notifications** — push notification or email: "Your scheduled post to Instagram was published" or "Failed — Instagram token expired, reconnect here." Buffer's #1 complaint is silent failures. We never leave you guessing.

37. **Consistency heatmap** — GitHub-style contribution graph showing which days you posted and which you didn't. Green squares for active days, empty for gaps. Motivates consistent posting. Unique — nobody in the social media space has this. Creators would screenshot and share it.

38. **Undo post** — 30-second window after posting to undo/delete the post from the platform. Like Gmail's "Undo Send." Everyone has posted something with a typo and panicked. This is a genuine life-saver and NO competitor has it.

39. **Platform health status** — small indicator showing if Instagram/YouTube/TikTok APIs are currently having issues. When a post fails, users blame the tool. If we show "Instagram API is experiencing delays right now" it builds trust instantly. Turns a negative moment into a trust-building moment.

40. **Saved hashtag groups** — save collections like "fitness hashtags", "travel hashtags", "brand hashtags." One click to insert a whole group into your caption. Saves creators from retyping the same 30 hashtags every post. Simple, powerful, time-saver.

41. **Cross-platform caption tweaks** — post to IG + YT + TikTok at once, but customize the caption for each platform individually. Same media, different captions. Currently every tool either posts identical text everywhere (bad) or makes you create separate posts (slow). We do both — shared media, per-platform captions.

42. **Post performance comparison** — posted the same video on YouTube AND Instagram? See side-by-side: "YouTube: 1.2k views, 45 likes | Instagram: 800 views, 120 likes." Helps creators understand which platform works best for which content. No competitor offers this cross-platform comparison view.

### IDEAS TO DEFINITELY SKIP

- ❌ Ad campaign management — too complex, not our market
- ❌ Learning portal / "Metrischool" — make the product self-explanatory instead
- ❌ Language switcher — browsers handle this
- ❌ "Explore" / content discovery — Publer's is useless, don't copy broken features
- ❌ SmartLinks — Later owns this space, not our fight
- ❌ RSS auto-posting — niche, confusing, adds complexity
